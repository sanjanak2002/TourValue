More About TourValue

TourValue is a software whose main objective is to enhance travel experience with the help of Blockchain.
For this, we plan to deal with four major concerns in this field. (Credits: Allerin)

  • - Customer/Passenger Identification
  • - Secure Transactions
  • - Baggage Management
  • - Customer Rewards and Ratings
